An introduction to new Salzburg students

Map purpose

The idea of this map is to provide students that are new in Salzburg with an interactive map where they can locate Cafés, Sport facilities and University amenities.

The following tab on the left provides an overview over the interactivity included in the map.

Interactivity

  1. Get to know Cafés in Salzburg

    2. The first interactive feature concerns the Cafés. By a simple click, the respective Café will greet the user including its name.

  2. Highlighting features

    3. To draw attention to the users pointer, the map features represented by a polygon (Sport areas & University amenities) are highlighted when hovering over them with the mouse.

  3. Tooltips, Popups and Zoom-In

    4. To facilitate the understanding of what the map features represent, hovering over them provides a short description of what can be found there. Clicking the features will open a box including more detailed information regarding what type of sport can be exercised here or what building of the University Salzburg is at a certain location.

    For polygon features, a zoom-in function is included. Right-clicking on the respective object triggers a smooth zooming towards a close-up of the feature.
